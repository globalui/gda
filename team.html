Meet The Team

  • India

Go Discover Abroad is all about letting you experience the beauty of traveling across the world, while living every moment to the fullest. Every person associated with the organization knows the value of exploring the unknown, keen to learn new things, and ready to share their experiences with all enthusiastic travelers who are contemplating about a gap year abroad!

Who Are We And Why We Love Traveling So Much?

Saurabh Sabharwal (CEO)

The founder and director of Go Discover Abroad, Saurabh is one of the most well-traveled persons you'll ever come across! He has traversed across 40 countries till date and there's absolutely no wonder why he came up with such a brilliant idea of offering adventure tours to enthusiast travelers like him, who are always on a quest of doing something out of the box!

He loves to travel because he feels that it relaxes his mind and the experiences he gathers on the road, helps him to be a better person. Also, it makes him aware of the bigger world, which one can never know while sitting at home! And Saurabh is an absolute foodie - he'd make sure to try the local cuisine and hop around the best eateries while traveling!

Sahil Sabharwal (COO)

Leading the Operations of the organization, coordinating with partner providers across the world, and offering expert guidance to enthusiastic travelers, Sahil has also explored the world extensively. His experiences in different parts of the world has encouraged him to design adventurous and meaningful tours for millennials seeking something interesting from their journey.!

He loves to travel because he truly believes that it’s something that makes a person richer. From his journeys, he has learned that experiences which you get out of travelling to any destination cannot be matched up to any other experience.

Namrata Jaiswal (Program Manager)

Being a Program Manager and Travel Expert, Namrata offers her guidance to travelers who are planning to step out of their comfort zone and experience the goodness of exploring a new country in some other part of the world. She's a perfectionist when it comes to working and extremely organized in whatever she does. When she's not working (or traveling), you'll mostly find her in the mall - a shopaholic she is!

She believes in the saying - "The world is a book and those who do not travel read only one page”. Thus to find new ways of living life by experiencing different cultures, savoring different cuisines, exploring nature's hidden trails, etc., are few of the factors that encourages her to travel more.

Dipti Goyal (Digital Marketing Manager)

Apart from being an expert in Digital Marketing, Dipti is also crazy for yoga and that is one of the biggest motivations which keeps her going! A learner by nature, she has been balancing her life between work & her passion and seeking to do better in both.

She loves to travel because traveling helps her to learn more about places and people, and also to get away from the chaos of life.

Riyanka Roy(Senior Content Writer)

Working as a content creator and blogger, Riyanka loves to juggle with words and narrate stories of her journeys. She is currently focusing on polishing her photography skills so that her words can be accompanied by some visual treats as well! Madly in love with the Himalayas, she's born with gypsy blood and can totally encourage someone to set yourself free to travel. She's always planning 'Where To Next?'

She loves to travel because life, she feels, is like a book and whatever we do adds more chapters to it. She enjoys writing about her experiences, and whatever comes her way, for she believes that each and every bit helps in making the chapters more colorful.

WHY GoDiscoverAbroad?

Connect with the world. Dive in to different cultures. Eat your heart out. Meet new people. Make a difference. Live a life less ordinary. Welcome to GDA. As group adventure travel experts, our passion for authentic, responsible and life changing travel experiences is what sets us apart from the rest. With us, you’ll travel with friends, leave with family, and see the world in a way you never thought possible.
